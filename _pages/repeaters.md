Frequencies
---

Our Repeaters are as follows:
- 2 M Analog [145.700 MHz]   (Echolink ZS6STN-R) 
- 2 M Dstar [145.5875 MHz]
- 70 CM DMR [438.35000 MHZ] (Color Code 1)
- 70 CM Analog [438.800 MHz]  (Echolink ZS6STN-L)
